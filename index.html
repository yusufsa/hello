Yusuf A

I think about and tinker with ways to empower more people as creatives.

I'm a grad student at the MIT Media Lab. I am currently developing a creative design tool for educators, exploring future models for learning, and experimenting with ways to bring open-ended, project-based learning online in response to COVID-19.

Before MIT, I helped launch a pan-African University network with sites in Mauritius, Rwanda, South Africa, and Kenya. We remixed ideas from Minerva, Ashesi, High Tech High, 42, and many others as we tried to create a new kind of university: hands on projects, research, and internships replaced lectures and problem sets. We've only graduated two classes, but our graduates are already making waves accross the continent.
